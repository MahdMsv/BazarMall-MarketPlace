Our Mission

We believe in the value of things made by hand

BazarMall was founded in 2022 with a simple idea: make it easy for skilled craftspeople to share what they make with the people who'll love it most.

The Story

From a small bazaar to a digital marketplace

It started at a local weekend market in 2021. Our founders kept noticing the same problem: talented artisans with extraordinary products, but no real way to reach customers beyond a few busy Saturdays a year.

We built BazarMall to give those makers a permanent, beautiful home online — without the complexity of running their own shop.
